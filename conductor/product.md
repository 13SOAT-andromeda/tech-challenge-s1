# Product Definition

## Initial Concept
A complete API for managing a mechanical workshop, developed in Go (Golang). It allows managing customers, vehicles, products, and service orders, with JWT authentication, Swagger documentation, and simplified deployment with Docker Compose.

## Vision
To provide a robust, scalable, and easy-to-maintain backend for mechanical workshop management, ensuring clear separation of concerns through Hexagonal Architecture and providing a seamless experience for both developers and end-users.

## Key Features
- **Customer Management:** CRUD operations for customers and their vehicles.
- **Service Order Management:** Creation and tracking of maintenance and service orders.
- **Product & Inventory:** Management of products used in services.
- **Authentication & Security:** Secure access using JWT and role-based permissions (admin setup).
- **API Documentation:** Interactive documentation via Swagger and Redoc.

## Target Users
- **Workshop Owners:** To manage the overall business operations.
- **Mechanics:** To track and update service orders.
- **Administrators:** To manage system configurations and user access.
